In the first weeks of Harvard's CS50 course computer science students learn about a technique called "Duck Debugging" in which you talk to a rubber duck when stuck solving a problem. You don't actually need to talk to a duck for this to work, but the general idea is that talking out loud about your code can help you solve a problem. In a similiar vain, I'd now like to invite you to choose a rubber duck in hopes it will help you on your coding journey and as you navigate this site.


Choose a duck!
