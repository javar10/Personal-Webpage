Education and Career

My education and career is one marked by many paths. I graduated from the University of Virginia with a bachelor's in architecture, but quickly decided that field wasn't for me. In search of something more altruistic, I joined the Peace Corps where I served as an English Teaching Volunteer in Panama. Upon completing my service, I continued in the field of education and became an ESOL teacher. After a decade in education, I happened upon the world of programming while tinkering in Arduino. From there, I had found a new passion. My current career goal is to become a software developer in the field of EdTech. I want to create engaging games for learners of all backgrounds so they can also discover their passions.
